Our Tools

The Vista Tools are a web-based suite of tools & resources to help insurance agents become more successful. There are 3 components that make up the Vista tools and they are available for the monthly subscription fee of $39.95

Vista Tracker

Vista Tracker is a CRM built especially for the insurance industry that allows you to see your sales pipeline clearly and take the actions that matter. It is fully customizable to fit your unique sales process.

IUL Prospecting System

The Vista Prospecting System was designed to educate the consumer on the IUL strategy. It combines social media, email & text campaigns with landing pages & educational webinars that accelerate the client through the sales process.

IUL Analyzer

The IUL Analyzer compares Qualified plans to the IUL strategy. It's simple for agents to use and prospects to understand. Enter your prospect's information, upload an illustration to the system and the IUL Analyzer provides a powerful timeline-based comparison.
